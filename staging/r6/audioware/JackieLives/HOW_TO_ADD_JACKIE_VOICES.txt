Jackie Lives — Jackie's real voice (OPTIONAL)
=============================================

By default this mod runs with SUBTITLES ONLY. Jackie's actual voice lines are
CD PROJEKT RED audio and cannot be redistributed, so they are NOT included in
this download. If you own Cyberpunk 2077 you can extract them yourself and drop
them in here to give Jackie his real voice.

WHAT THIS FOLDER IS
-------------------
  r6\audioware\JackieLives\
    JackieLives.yml   <- the Audioware sound bank (lists every "jl_<id>" clip)
    (audio files)     <- YOU add these; each must match a filename in the .yml

HOW TO ADD THE VOICES
---------------------
Follow the video tutorial (link on the mod's Nexus page) — it walks through the
WolvenKit extraction step by step. In short:
  1. Install WolvenKit + this mod's normal requirements (RED4ext, CET, Codeware,
     Audioware, AMM — see the Nexus description).
  2. Use WolvenKit to extract Jackie's voice-over files and convert them to the
     filenames listed in JackieLives.yml, into THIS folder.
  3. Launch the game — Audioware loads the bank and Jackie speaks his lines.

Without the audio files the mod still works perfectly — you just read his lines
instead of hearing them. Nothing here will crash if the audio is missing.
